## Link Quality Checklist

**Every link should:**
- ✅ Come from a relevant website (related to real estate, commerce, Ohio, etc.)
- ✅ Be natural (not paid/artificial)
- ✅ Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- ✅ Come from a page with existing authority
- ✅ Be from a different domain (diversify)
- ✅ Not be spammy or from link farms

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Buying links (violates Google guidelines)
- ❌ Link exchanges (not valuable)
- ❌ Irrelevant sites (hurts authority)
- ❌ Links from spam directories

## Key Success Factors

1. **Consistency:** Aim for 2-3 links per week (sustainable)
2. **Quality over quantity:** 10 high-authority links beat 100 spam links
3. **Relevance:** Links from Ohio/real estate/business sites > random links
4. **Patience:** SEO takes 3-6 months to show real results
5. **Content:** Good content is easier to link to and share
6. **Follow-up:** Track relationships, nurture partnerships long-term
