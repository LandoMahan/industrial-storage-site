## What I Need From You

### Property Details
1. **Address** — Full address in Grove City, OH
2. **Square Footage** — Total usable space on second floor
3. **Ceiling Height** — Vertical clearance (ft)
4. **Loading Access** — Details (truck dock, elevator, ramp, etc.)
5. **Climate Control** — Yes/No, and any specifics (temperature range, humidity control, etc.)
6. **Security** — Features (cameras, alarm, locked access, etc.)
7. **Lighting** — Natural/LED/other details

### Business Details
8. **Pricing** — $/sqft/month or flat monthly rate?
9. **Minimum Lease** — Shortest acceptable lease term?
10. **Contact Info** — Phone number and email for inquiries
11. **Company Name** — For branding (if not just "Industrial Storage")
12. **Service Hours** — Access hours (24/7 or business hours?)

### Content & Marketing
13. **Unique Selling Points** — Any specific advantages? (location, accessibility, features)
14. **Target Audience** — Specific types of contractors? Equipment? Materials?
15. **Photos/Images** — Do you have photos of the space? (I can add placeholders or design mockups if not)
16. **Additional Services** — Anything beyond storage? (Loading dock rental, material lift, etc.)

## Deployment Options

### Simple (Static Site)
- Host on Netlify or Vercel (free)
- Just HTML/CSS/JS, no backend
- Form submits to email service
- Takes 5 minutes to deploy

### Full Stack (Email + Database)
- Node.js backend for form handling
- SQLite or PostgreSQL for lead storage
- Email notifications
- Admin dashboard to view/manage inquiries
- Takes 30 minutes to set up

### Custom Domain
- Point your own domain to the site
- Set up SSL/HTTPS
- Custom email (hello@yourdomain.com)
