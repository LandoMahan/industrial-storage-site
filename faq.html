Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about lease terms, access, climate control, insurance, and more.

Q: What types of businesses use your storage?

We serve all kinds — contractors storing equipment and vehicles, retail shops needing backstock space, small manufacturers storing inventory, businesses outgrowing their current location, and entrepreneurs who need flexible, cost-effective space without a 3-year commitment. If you need climate-controlled, secure, accessible storage, we're built for you.

Q: Do you offer month-to-month leases?

Yes. Month-to-month is our default option. No long-term lockup, no penalty for leaving (just 30 days notice). We also offer 6-month and 12-month leases with small discounts if you want predictability. You control the terms.

Q: What are your access hours?

Standard access is 6 AM - 10 PM, seven days a week. If you need after-hours emergency access, we can arrange that with advance notice and proper security procedures. We're here when you need us.

Q: Is there loading dock access or drive-in space?

Yes. We have ground-level drive-in access with a loading area. Plus, our 12,000 lb freight elevator handles loaded pallets, equipment, and vehicles. Whether you're moving pallets or parking vehicles, we've got the infrastructure.

Q: Can I expand or reduce my space mid-lease?

Absolutely. If your business grows, we can add adjacent space (subject to availability). If you need less, we can negotiate a lease modification. We're flexible because your business needs change — we understand that.

Q: What's your climate control situation?

Temperature is maintained between 55-75°F and humidity is controlled at 35-65% relative humidity. This is ideal for sensitive inventory, equipment, documents, or anything that shouldn't experience extreme temperature swings. Climate control is included in your lease.

Q: Do you allow hazardous materials?

No. We don't permit flammable, explosive, or hazardous materials per safety and insurance requirements. Our lease outlines specific restrictions. Call (610) 297-0112 if you're unsure whether your materials qualify.

Q: How long does it take to move in after signing a lease?

Typically 3-5 business days after lease is signed and move-in costs are paid. We need time to process paperwork, set up your access card, and conduct a final facility walkthrough. We'll give you a specific date when you sign.

Q: What's your cancellation policy?

Month-to-month leases: Cancel anytime with 30 days written notice. No penalty. 6-month or 12-month leases: Early exit requires 1 month's rent as a fee. This is standard and protects both of us. Lease will detail this clearly.

Q: Do I need my own insurance?

Yes. We require proof of liability insurance naming 2882 Storage as an additional insured. This typically costs $200-400/year through your business insurance provider. It's standard for industrial space and protects you as much as us.

Q: Can I expand into multiple spaces if I grow?

Yes. If you start with 1,000 sqft and need 5,000 sqft in 6 months, we can expand your lease to adjacent spaces (subject to availability). Growth is good — we work with you to scale.

Q: What if I need access outside of 6 AM - 10 PM hours?

Contact us at (610) 297-0112 and we'll discuss your needs. Emergency after-hours access is possible with advance notice and security procedures. There may be additional fees depending on frequency.
